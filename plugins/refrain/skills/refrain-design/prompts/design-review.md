# Design Review Prompt Contract

When reviewing an interface, lead with issues. Judge the real surface, not the
intention.

## Review Order

1. first-viewport operationality
2. scan hierarchy
3. token and accent discipline
4. surface treatment
5. control ergonomics
6. empty/loading/error/populated states
7. visual previews for visual choices
8. accessibility and reduced motion
9. product-specific detail

## Output Shape

- Findings first, ordered by severity.
- Give concrete file or surface references when available.
- Then list missing verification.
- Keep praise out unless it explains a decision to preserve.
