# Surface

Refrain surfaces are quiet, bordered, and inspectable. They can be dense without
becoming noisy.

## Rules

- Separate in-page surfaces with border and spacing first.
- Use shadows for popovers, menus, dialogs, and overlays.
- Keep records, previews, dialogs, and framed tools as cards.
- Do not use cards as page-section decoration.
- Do not nest cards.
- Make hit targets stable even when visual separators are thin.

## Useful Treatments

- flat fills for panes and rows
- subtle separators for secondary grouping
- dashed or tinted canvas only when a surface is waiting for content
- themed scrollbars that do not dominate the page

## Reject

- shadow-heavy card grids
- decorative bokeh, blobs, glow, or orbits
- rounded left-accent dashboard cards
- stock SaaS panels that do not expose real product state
